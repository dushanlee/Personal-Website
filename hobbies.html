Hobbies & Interests

Outside of coding, I enjoy cooking, playing multiple instruments, badminton, and volunteering on missions with my church.

Cooking

I love exploring new cuisines and techniques, especially cooking steak, burgers, Korean, Mexican, and Indian dishes and enjoy learning new recipes from different cultures.

Playing Guitar

I play guitar and sing for fun. I serve on my church’s worship team for Friday and Sunday services and enjoy teaching others how to play.

Playing Drums

I play drums to relieve stress and enjoy being the one who keeps the rhythm in a band. I also serve on my church’s worship team for Sunday services and love teaching others.

Badminton

I play badminton to exercise, stay active, and maintain my health. As a racquet sport, it’s excellent for hand–eye coordination and reaction time, encourages friendly competition, and has been linked with longevity in population studies.

Church Missions

I serve on church missions overseas, speaking Spanish with local communities and supporting practical needs through outreach. These experiences build empathy and perspective, grounding my work and goals. I feel called to do the Lord’s work with humility, teamwork, and care.

Playing Video Games

I enjoy playing video games with friends to relax, have fun, and stay connected. Gaming is a great way to unwind, challenge myself, and share memorable moments and teamwork with others, whether it’s through friendly competition or cooperative adventures.
