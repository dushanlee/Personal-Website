About Me

Hi! My name is Dushan (Michael) Lee. My journey into tech began in an unexpected place: medicine. I was originally on the pre-med path, but I found myself drawn to the problem-solving, systems-thinking, and creative process that software development offers. That shift led me to complete Hack Reactor’s 16-week full-stack software engineering bootcamp, where I gained hands-on experience with JavaScript, Python, React, and building full-stack web applications.

I’m passionate about building clean, intuitive tools that make life easier and more efficient. I thrive in collaborative environments, value thoughtful, maintainable code, and enjoy learning through doing. I'm excited to keep learning, building, and growing alongside others who care about real collaboration and continuous improvement. Outside of tech, I’m into gaming, badminton, cooking, music, and exploring how people think and grow, and most importantly, thinking about the Roman Empire.
